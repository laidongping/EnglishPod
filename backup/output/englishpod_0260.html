Key Vocabulary

targetobject to be aimed at in shooting practice
revolvera small gun with a container for bullets
inspectto look at (something) carefully

Supplementary Vocabulary

riflea shoulder firearm with spiral grooves cut in the inner surface of the gun barrel
scopeinstrument on a gun for viewing and aiming at the target
magazinea metal receptacle for a number of cartridges, inserted into certain types of automatic weapons
roundammunition for a single shot
barrelthe metal, cylindrical part of a firearm through which the bullet travels
