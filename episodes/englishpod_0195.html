C0195 Job Hunting #Global View

A
Woo hoo! This just might be the start of the rest of my life!
B
What happened?
A
I’m in the market for a job! I went on a website with hundreds of job listings in the area and browsed through them until I got the names of a few employers I would like to work for. I have the resume I wrote for English class last month and a cover letter will be a piece of cake to write. I’ve even done my research and found the names of the managers so I can address the letters personally. And you know I can be charming in interviews. Goodbye my penniless days! Hello salary and a career!
B
Ben, we’re fifteen. What kind of job are you looking for?
A
Oh, just for a position as a gas station attendant. You know, starting at a simple lowly job, just like all the greats before they made it big in the world.
B
Uh-huh.
A
But I’m just in it for the money, right? How else am I going to be able to afford to keep taking Angela to the movies? Besides, I love the smell of gasoline, don’t you?

Key Vocabulary

manager common noun, plural person who manages an organization or company
charming Adjective delightful; pleasant; attractive
interview common noun, plural a conversation with or questioning of a person
penniless Adjective very poor; almost totally without money
salary common noun, singular money paid by the employer each month
career common noun, singular profession that someone does for a long time
position common noun, singular job, occupation
gas station common noun, singular place where gasoline is sold
attendant common noun, singular a person employed to provide a service
lowly Adjective humble or low in position
greats common noun, plural person with importance or distinction in their field
uh-huh Interjection a less emphatic variant of yes
gasoline common noun, non-variable petroleum fuel
browse principle verb, past simple to search for and read hypertext
employer common noun, plural a person or firm who employs
resume common noun, singular a summary of your academic and work history
address principle verb, present simple to direct a speech or written statement to someone
job listing phrase database containing an ordered array of job items
a piece of cake phrase something is easily to be done
make it big phrase make a great success
in the market phrase be interested in buying
woo hoo Interjection exclamation of excitement

Supplementary Vocabulary

Headhunter common noun, singular an employment recruiter, sometimes referred to as executive search.
Job Agency common noun, singular A company that helps both employers and job applicants find a position.
CV common noun, singular Curriculum Vitae; a summary of academic and professional history and achievements
No Brainer phrase something that is easy or not challenging
Easy As Pie phrase something that is easy or not challenging
