Supplementary Vocabulary

pesticide N strong chemical used to kill bugs and pests
flea N a small insect that is usually found in hairy animals like cats and dogs
mouse trap N device used to catch or kill mice
fly swatter N a hand-held device for swatting flies and other insects
roach motel N small device where cockroaches can enter and eat poisoned food
