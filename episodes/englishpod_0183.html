Supplementary Vocabulary

snowsuit common noun, singular a oneor two-piece outer garment for cold weather, often consisting of heavily lined pants and jacket
gloves common noun, plural hand covering separating all fingers and thumb
coat common noun, singular an outer garment with sleeves, covering at least the upper part of the body
mitts common noun, plural short for mittens
boots common noun, plural a covering of leather, rubber, or the like, for the foot and all or part of the leg
