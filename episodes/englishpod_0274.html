amount to P to turn out to be

stand V represent

cutthroat A situation in which people compete in an unpleasant way

Supplementary Vocabulary

pie chart N a circular chart divided into sectors, illustrating proportion
bar graph N a chart with rectangular bars with lengths proportional to the values that they represent.
line chart N a two-dimensional scatterplot of ordered observations where the observations are connected following their order.
market share N the proportion of industry sales of a good or service that is controlled by a company.
sales forecast N the estimate of upcoming or future sales
