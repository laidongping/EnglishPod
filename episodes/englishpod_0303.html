Supplementary Vocabulary

taco N crisply fried tortilla folded over and filled
tortilla N a kind of thin pancake made from corn, cooked on a hot griddle until dry
dip N a liquid or soft substance into which something is dipped
Churro N a Spanish and Mexican pastry resembling a doughnut or cruller and made from deepfried dough
