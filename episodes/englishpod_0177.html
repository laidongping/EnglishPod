Supplementary Vocabulary

tinsel common noun, non-variable very thin strips of shiny metallic material
garland common noun, singular a chain of flowers, leaves, or tinsel
wreath common noun, singular a closed circle of garland
light up the Christmas tree phrase turn on the Christmas tree lights
Christmas star/angel common noun, singular a large ornament in the shape of a star or an angel, usually with lights, placed on the very top of the Christmas tree
