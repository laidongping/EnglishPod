C0357 Making A Dinner Reservation #Daily Life

A
Bruno Bistro, how may I help you?
B
Yes hello, I would like to make a reservation please.
A
Certainly sir, For which day and time please?
B
Tonight at seven.
A
I’m sorry sir, but we are fully booked tonight until eight.
B
In that case, eight o’clock is fine.
A
Very well, and how many people will attend tonight?
B
Four people.
A
Lastly, may I please know what name I should make the reservation under?
A
Mark.

Key Vocabulary

bistro N mid-priced restaurant
make a reservation P to make an arrangement to hold something for later
fully booked P all sold out or full of reservations
in that case P if something said earlier is true
attend V go to
reservation N Something that is kept back or withheld

Supplementary Vocabulary

steakhouse N a restaurant that specializes in beef steaks.
diner N A place that offers a wide range of foods, mostly American, a casual atmosphere and late operating hours.
Soup Kitchen N a place where food is offered to the hungry for free or at a reasonably low price
saltshaker N a container with a perforated top for sprinkling salt
Cutlery N Utensils such as knives, forks, and spoons used as tableware
