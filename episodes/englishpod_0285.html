Supplementary Vocabulary

plumber N someone who installs the pipes, bathroom fixtures, and fixes toilets, sinks, etc.
electrician N someone who works with the electric wiring of a building
plunger N a rubber device that helps to unclog toilets
insulation N material used to regulate temperature; keep a building warm in the winter and cool in the summer
gutter N a channel on the side of a building or street that guides away surface water
