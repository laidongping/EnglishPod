Key Vocabulary

grip principle verb, infinitive hold, reach
clutch common noun, singular the pedal that you press before you change gear
sidewalk common noun, singular the part of the road where people walk
steering wheel common noun, singular the wheel which the driver holds in a car
pull over principle verb, present simple to move a car to the side of the road and stop

Supplementary Vocabulary

seatbelt common noun, singular a belt in a car fastened around the midsection to keep the person safely secured against the seat
speed limit common noun, singular the maximum speed allowed on the road
intersection common noun, singular a place where two or more major roads meet
traffic light common noun, singular a set of electrically operated signal lights used to direct or control traffic at intersections
yield sign common noun, singular a road sign in the shape of an upside-down triangle, meaning to slow down and prepare to stop if necessary
