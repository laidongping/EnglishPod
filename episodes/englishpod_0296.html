C0296 Vegan Or Vegetarian? #Global View

A
Hey Julie, you want to go grab something to eat?
B
Sure! What do you feel like having?
A
I really feel like having a big juicy steak!
B
Oh. ok. I don’t eat meat, but that’s fine, I am sure wherever we are going they will have other options right?
A
I didn’t know you were a vegetarian!
B
I’m not, I am a vegan.
A
A what?
B
A vegan. I don’t eat or use any animal based products. I don’t wear leather, eat eggs, drink milk or anything that comes from an animal. I used to be a pescatarian before, which basically means you don’t eat meat, but still have fish and seafood.
A
Wow! That’s interesting! It must be tough!
B
It’s a bit difficult to find vegetarian friendly restaurants sometimes, but since more and more people are vegetarians or vegans nowadays, it’s getting a bit less difficult.

Key Vocabulary

feel like P have an inclination or desire for
steak N piece of cow meat
vegetarian N a person who does not eat meat or fish
vegan N a person who does not eat or use animal products
leather A made with animal skin that is chemically treated
come from P originates from
more and more P continuing to become larger in number

Supplementary Vocabulary

vegetable N kind of food that is not meat
meat N kind of food that is not a vegetable
milk N substance coming from a cow
hippie N a person whose behavior, dress, etc, implied a rejection of conventional values
