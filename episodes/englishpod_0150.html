Supplementary Vocabulary

mosquito net common noun, singular a net placed usually around a bed to prevent somone sleeping from being bitten
rash common noun, singular an outbreak of spots or patches on the skin, caused by illness or allergy
malaria common noun, singular a disease with recurring attacks of fever, caused by the bite of some types of mosquito
bug zapper common noun, singular a device that attracts and kills insects that are attracted by light
pesticide common noun, singular a chemical used to destroy pests, esp. insects
