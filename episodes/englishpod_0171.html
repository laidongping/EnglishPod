Key Vocabulary

pointand- shoot Adjective Any compact non-SLR camera with noninterchangeable lenses, using automatic settings and simplified controls. Generally desired by novices and casual users.
interest someone in phrase try to persuade someone to buy, do, or eat something
lens common noun, singular a transparent optical device used to converge or diverge transmitted light and to form images
polarizing filter phrase a camera lens filter used to control the plane of polarization of light entering the lens.
entrylevel Adjective simple and appropriate to an inexperienced person

Supplementary Vocabulary

tripod common noun, singular a three-legged stand for a camera
filter common noun, singular a lens screen of dyed gelatin or glass for controlling color or for diminishing the intensity of light
exposure common noun, singular the process of light hitting a photosensitive surface, such as film or a digital image sensor
shutter common noun, singular a mechanical device for opening and closing the aperture of a camera lens to expose film
wideangle lens common noun, singular a lens which has a wide coverage angle so it can take in a great deal of scene, usually any lens shorter than 42mm
