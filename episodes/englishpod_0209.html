C0209 Office Gossip #Daily Life

Pam
Psssst! Pssssssssst! Hey! Eric, have you heard?
Eric
Hm? No… go on, tell me, what’s the latest office gossip?
Pam
Well, you didn’t hear this from me but the rumor is… … … . is getting a promotion!
Eric
No way! But… she’s a terrible worker… and you can’t trust her… she’s so two-faced - you can’t believe anything she says!
Paula
Hey guys, what are you two whispering about?
Eric
Oh Hi Paula! How are you?
Paula
I’ve got some good news! I’m getting a promotion!
Pam
Congratulations! Eric and I were just saying that you are the best person forthe job… .
Eric
Yes! You’re the best!

Key Vocabulary

hear principle verb, present perfect learn something by the ear (usually in surprise)
gossip common noun, non-variable rumor or talk of a personal, sensational nature
whisper principle verb, present progressive soft speech produced without full voice
rumor common noun, singular a mixture of truth and untruth passed around
promotion common noun, singular act of raising in rank or position

Supplementary Vocabulary

talk common noun, singular someone who cannot keep a secret
trust Adjective someone who cannot be trusted
praise principle verb, present simple to speak very well about someone
bad mouth common noun, singular to speak poorly about someone
Lie common noun, singular someone who does not tell the truth
