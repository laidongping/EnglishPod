Supplementary Vocabulary

Collar N the cloth around the neckline of a shirt, that is folded over
Impression N the first feeling you give when you encounter/meet someone
Custommade A made according to your own specifications
Alteration N a change (in length, width, fit) made to an item of clothing
Dry- Clean V A cleaning process that uses chemicals to clean clothes
Herringbone A A type of pattern, common on suits, that resembles thin, “sawlike” lines
Garment N an article of clothing
