Supplementary Vocabulary

Angel Investors common noun, singular Individuals who back emerging entrepreneurial ventures, usually as a bridge to get from the selffunded stage to the level of business that would both need and attract venture capital.
Joint Venture common noun, singular A legal entity created by two or more businesses joining together to conduct a specific business enterprise with both parties sharing profits and losses.
Venture Capital common noun, singular A form of financing for a company in which the business gives up partial ownership and control of the business in exchange for capital over a limited time frame, usually 3-5 years.
Merger common noun, singular A joining together of two previously separate corporations.
Audit common noun, singular an evaluation of a person, organization, system, process, enterprise, project or product.
