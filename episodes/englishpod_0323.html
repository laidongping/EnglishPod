C0323 Cancelled Flight #Daily Life

A
Good afternoon Sir, may I please see your passport and reservation?
B
Here you go.
A
I’m sorry sir, this flight has been cancelled due to some mechanical problems.
B
Cancelled! So what am I supposed to do now?
A
We apologize for any inconveniences that may be caused by this. If your flight is urgent, I can put you on a waiting list for another flight this evening, but it’s on a first come first served basis, so there is no guarantee that you will be able to take that flight.
B
What’s my other option?
A
If you can wait until tomorrow, we will put youup in a hotel for today and you can take scheduled flight for tomorrow morning.
B
That’s fine. I’ll do that then.
A
Thank you for your understanding sir. I will book your flight now.

Key Vocabulary

reservation N an arrangement to secure accommodations at a restaurant or hotel
cancel V to decide that something will not happen
inconvenience N he quality or state of being inconvenient.
urgent A important
scheduled V planned at a specific time

Supplementary Vocabulary

route N a customary or regular line of passage or travel
