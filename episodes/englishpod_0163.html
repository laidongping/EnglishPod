Supplementary Vocabulary

pensionmoney paid at regular intervals from the government or a previous employer to a person following retirement or to their surviving dependents
disability income insurancefinancial assistance to people who acquire a disability so that they can maintain a certain income level
survivor benefitsfinancial assistance for the families of workers who die
unemployment benefits/ compensationpayments made to an unemployed worker by the goverment or employer during all or part of the period of unemployment
defined benefit plana pension plan that pays a defined pension/benefit, but the amount invested may vary
defined contribution plana pension plan that has a defined amount to invest, but whose pension/benefit depends on the success of those investments
