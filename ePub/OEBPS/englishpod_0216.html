C0216 Daily Life - Health Food

IntermediateDaily LifeC0216
John
Ok darling, got some pizzas, potato chips, hot dogs and lots of cheese!
Kelly
Oh John, I thought we said we would start eating right! Remember? Our new healthy lifestyle? That’s all junk food!
John
Hrumph! Right, so what did you get?
Kelly
Well, healthy food , of course! I got some whole wheat bread, skimmed milk, fresh fish and organic carrots….
John
Organic? What’s organic? Do we need organic carrots…?
Kelly
They were grown without using any chemicals that are harmful to our health. And yes, John, we need organic carrots….
John
Oh, so organic vegetables are the ‘green’ option, right?
Kelly
Yup, better for the environment and better for us!
John
Wait a minute, that? …Doughnuts? they organic doughnuts, Kelly?
Kelly
…. I like doughnuts.
